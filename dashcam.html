About Us

Welcome to Dashcam Store, your number one source for all things dashcam. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of dashcams, with a focus on quality, customer service, and reliability.

Founded in 2020 by Alex Johnson, Dashcam Store has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When Alex first started out, his passion for helping other drivers be safe on the road drove him to quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the US, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the automotive industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely, Alex Johnson, Founder
